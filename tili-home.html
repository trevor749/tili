About The Pilot

Built For The Athletes
Long Island Already Produces.

A first-of-its-kind launch from Athletes Make The Best™, Long Island Media, and The SPIN Foundation. Four beats. Plain English. No spin.

01 / The Problem

Long Island produces some of the best young athletes in the country. They train. They compete. They graduate. Then most disappear into careers far from the discipline that built them.

02 / The Solution

The Workforce Studio turns Long Island student-athletes into paid content creators, brand ambassadors, and on-camera operators for the businesses and schools that want to reach them.

03 / The Studio

Every studio day is run by Long Island Media, the same broadcast crew behind work for Chase, Forbes, and DreamSeat. Real equipment. Real direction. Real athletes.

04 / The Outcome

Athletes get paid. Brands get content that converts. Schools get a workforce pipeline. Long Island companies reach the audience next door. The whole island wins.
