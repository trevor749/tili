About The Pilot

Built For The Athletes
Long Island Already Produces.

A first-of-its-kind launch from Athletes Make The Best™, Long Island Media, and The SPIN Foundation. Here is what that means in plain English.

Long Island produces some of the best young athletes in the country. They train all year. They post highlights. They graduate. And then most of them disappear into careers that have nothing to do with the discipline that made them. That is a leak in the pipeline. We are closing it.
When a brand books the studio, the team behind Long Island Media (the same crew that has produced for Chase, Forbes, and DreamSeat) runs a full broadcast-grade shoot day with hand-picked student-athletes on camera. Your brand. Their authenticity. Real content that runs everywhere.

The Workforce Studio turns Long Island student-athletes into paid content creators, brand ambassadors, and on-camera operators for the businesses and sponsors who want to reach them.

Athletes get paid. Brands get content that converts. Schools get a workforce pipeline. Long Island wins.
