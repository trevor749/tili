About The Pilot

Built For The Athletes
Long Island Already Produces.

A first-of-its-kind launch from Athletes Make The Best™, Long Island Media, and The SPIN Foundation. Here is what that means in plain English.

Long Island produces some of the best young athletes in the country. They train all year. They post highlights. They graduate. And then most disappear into careers that have nothing to do with the discipline that made them.

The Workforce Studio turns Long Island student-athletes into paid content creators for the brands that want to reach them.

Athletes get paid. Brands get content. Schools get a pipeline. Long Island wins.

When a brand books the studio, the team behind Long Island Media (same crew behind work for Chase, Forbes, and DreamSeat) runs a full broadcast-grade shoot day with hand-picked student-athletes on camera.
