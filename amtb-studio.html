The Pilot

Built For The Student-Athletes Long Island Already Produces.

Athletes Make The Best™ is launching the first Student-Athlete Workforce Studio of its kind, in partnership with Long Island Media and The SPIN Foundation. Here is what that means in plain English.

Long Island produces some of the best young athletes in the country. They train all year. They post highlights. They graduate. And then most of them disappear into careers that have nothing to do with the discipline that made them. That is a leak in the pipeline. We are closing it.
The Workforce Studio turns Long Island student-athletes into paid content creators, brand ambassadors, and on-camera operators for the businesses, schools, and sponsors who want to reach them.
When a brand books a studio day, the team behind Long Island Media (the same crew that has produced for Chase, Forbes, and DreamSeat) runs a full broadcast-grade shoot day with hand-picked student-athletes on camera. Your brand. Their authenticity. Real content that runs everywhere.
Athletes get paid. Brands get content that converts. Schools get a workforce pipeline. Long Island wins.

Questions People Ask

The Most Common First Questions.

What does the $2,960 deposit actually cover?
The deposit secures your studio date on our production calendar and triggers casting plus creative kickoff. It is credited in full toward your total project investment. Balance is invoiced after the creative scope is finalized.
How is the deposit paid?
You will receive an invoice from QuickBooks at the email you provide. Pay by credit card, ACH, or wire transfer through the secure QuickBooks payment portal. Your date is locked the moment payment clears.
What is the total project investment beyond the deposit?
Total varies by scope. Single-brand studio days typically run between $7,500 and $18,000 all-in, depending on athlete roster size, content volume, and distribution package. Final number is confirmed at the kickoff call.
Can I cancel or reschedule?
Yes. The deposit is refundable up to 14 days before your scheduled studio date. Inside 14 days, the deposit becomes non-refundable but can be applied to a rescheduled date within 90 days.
Who are the student-athletes?
Long Island student-athletes vetted and managed by Athletes Make The Best™. They are matched to your brand by sport, audience, and content style. You approve the final roster before production.
Do the athletes get paid?
Yes. Every student-athlete on set is compensated for their time and the licensing of their image and likeness. That is the entire point of the workforce studio.
Where does the studio operate?
The Long Island Media studio space, located in Nassau or Suffolk County. Exact address is shared at booking confirmation.
